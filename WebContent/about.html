ABOUT
THE BAND

JESSE is a band with their musical aspirations rooted in mainly jazz, funk, and any other crazy music that includes insane piano solos and trumpet-blowing nonsense. The band has its humble beginnings in the outskirts of Pohang in Handong Global University, where there is literally nothing to do except bang on the drums and blow the sax until your lungs run dry. This accounts for why the band was so successful comparatively to other "musical" ensembles located in Pohang and in fact, any other bands in Korea (without pertaining to bands pursuing a musical degree). Members of JESSE are also very confident in their skills (except for probably the vocalist) and like to brag about how fast their fingers move or about how powerful a solo was; though ironically, they only do this amongst themselves and do not protrude these remarks to other standerbys, retaining their natural humble ambience. If you wish to contact JESSE in regards to mini concerts and shows, please contact anyone below except the female members as they were taught well on how to deal with stranger danger and will not respond to any DMs or messages
